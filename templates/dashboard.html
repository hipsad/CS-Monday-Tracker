📊 How is the Problem Score calculated?

The Problem Score is a composite penalty metric — higher = more problematic.
It is calculated as: score = (1 / avg_rating) + (1 / avg_kd)
Players with a low Leetify Rating and low K/D will receive a higher score. A score above 3.0 is flagged as high-concern, above 1.5 as moderate. Only games with valid data are included in the averages.
